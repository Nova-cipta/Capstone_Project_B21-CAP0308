
    #Simple_Problem_Statement
    
    masalah : seseorang yang mengalami kekerasan (verbal, emotional, physical, sexual) 
              takut untuk mengadukan tindakan tersebut ke orang lain atau pihak terkait, karena 
              merasa terancam oleh pelaku kekerasan atau khawatir mendapat persepsi buruk terhadap pelaku kekerasan dari masyarakat.
              
    problem : Someone who had the violence (emotional, physical, verbal, sexual) tend to be afraid to speak out to other people such as other family, friends, 
              closest neighbors or related organizations, because they are feeling threatened by the person who did it 
              or moreover they will get bad perspective from others people.
              
    
    #Simple_Research_Questions
	
	1. 	Bagaimana sebuah sistem chatbot dapat membantu mengambil laporan dari seseorang yang mengalami kekerasan secara langsung ke pihak terkait
	    dengan efektif, rahasia, dan cepat?
		
	1. 	How chatbot can handle reports from people who had a violence directly to the related organizations with effective, securely and reliable fast
